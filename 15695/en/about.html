Company Profile

Current Location:Home>About Us>Company Profile

Wenzhou triple Forging Co., Ltd. was established in 1993, is privately held. Now in Wenzhou, Wuhu, Anhui, respectively built two plants, mainly the production of automotive components and wind power products.

The factory is located in Ruian, Wenzhou, founded in 1993, the company covers an area of ​​30,000 square meters, more than 400 employees. Is a professional production and processing of automotive forgings manufacturer, the company has advanced CNC molding equipment, mold design, heat treatment, and the implementation of a complete physical and chemical testing center.

Investment in Wuhu, Anhui High-tech Development Zone in 2006, triple the construction of new factories, the company covers an area of ​​51,500 square meters, has been the investment of 90 million yuan. Professional production of automotive parts, chassis parts, and wind power accessories, is now seven forging production line. The tonnage of production equipment from 600T to 2500T, hot forging and high-speed screw press, and each production line is equipped with temperature control induction furnace. In addition, the plant has advanced testing equipment, is equipped with Germany imported spectrometer and automatic imported from Germany detection machine. Wuhu triple production now has reached 25000T, and matching with the five hundred companies in the world, such as: Robert Bosch GmbH of Germany; Germany FAG; NTN Corporation of Japan: Beijing Hyundai, Shanghai Huizhong company, Anhui Chery Automobile Company; Hebei Great Wall Industry Corporation; Chongqing Changan.

Wenzhou triple and Wuhu triple the company pass the ISO/TS16949: 2002 quality system certification. Triple the company has always regarded customer satisfaction is our eternal pursuit! Unity and hard work, innovation, triple the company to become the first-class enterprise efforts in forging field.
