How to change main color?

Changing main color of the template is quite simple. Just open defaultcolor.css file and replace the color with your own. Kreator comes with few predefined color styles so you can use one of them too. All icons that are in color are made with font icons so there's no need of coloring icons in image editors. Everything is done in just one click.

How to make my site retina ready?

Template is using retina.js script which replaces all of your images that are placed in HTML code. You need to make two sizes of images. First, default one that will be displayed on normal screens will be named image.jpg, for example and retina image will be named image@2x.jpg. Images needs to be placed in the same folder.

Is contact form working?

Of course it does. All forms are working, including blog comments form. All you need to do is to enter your email in contact.php file. Email will be sent to your email address. Regarding blog comments, email will also be sent on your email address. You will need to enter the comment on your site manually.

Where can I download images?

All images were bought on 123rf.com. You can find link to all images used in this template in documentation, section "Sources and credits".
